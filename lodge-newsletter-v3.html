Masonic Light

"The Symbolism of the Three Steps"

Brother Marcus Brown delivered an illuminating presentation on the three steps leading to the East, representing the three stages of human life: Youth, with its vigor and learning; Manhood, with its strength and purpose; and Age, with its wisdom and reflection. The lecture reminded us that each stage offers unique opportunities for service to the Craft.
